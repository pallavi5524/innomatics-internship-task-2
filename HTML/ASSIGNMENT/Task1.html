Manage Your Finances with Ease

A simple personal budget tracker helps you manage your finances by keeping track of your income and expenses.
You start by entering your total income and then log your daily or monthly expenses into different categories
such as food, rent, transportation, and entertainment. The tracker automatically calculates the remaining balance
and provides insights into your spending habits. Some budget trackers also include visual charts or reports to
help you analyze where your money is going. By regularly updating your expenses, you can set financial goals,
avoid overspending, and maintain better control over your budget.
